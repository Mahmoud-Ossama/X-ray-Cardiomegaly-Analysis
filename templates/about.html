About This Project

What is Cardiomegaly?

Cardiomegaly refers to an enlarged heart, which can be a sign of several conditions including:

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart valve problems
  • Cardiomyopathy (a disease of the heart muscle)
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Congenital heart defects

Early detection of cardiomegaly can lead to improved patient outcomes through timely medical intervention.

AI in Medical Imaging

Artificial Intelligence has shown promising results in assisting radiologists and medical professionals in the analysis of medical images. Deep learning models can be trained to identify patterns in X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans that might indicate various medical conditions.

Benefits of AI in medical imaging include:

  • Faster preliminary screening
  • Assistance to radiologists in high-volume environments
  • Potential for increased diagnostic accuracy
  • Standardized analysis across different healthcare settings

About This Application

This Cardiomegaly Classifier is a project developed for the AI College Bioinformatics Department. It demonstrates how deep learning can be applied to medical image analysis.

The application uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on chest X-ray images to identify signs of heart enlargement. The model was trained using the NIH Chest X-ray dataset.

Important: This application is for educational and research purposes only and should not be used for clinical diagnosis.
